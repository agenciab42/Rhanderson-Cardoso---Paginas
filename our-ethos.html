Why Is Our Program Expensive?

At the Meta-Analysis Academy, we receive many inquiries about the pricing of our program. We believe in addressing this openly.

A big portion of our content (YouTube videos, eBooks and live sessions on research topics) are offered free of charge to anyone with internet access. This allows us to fulfil our mission of making this knowledge accessible worldwide. However, our advanced program teaching the methodology and direct application of meta-analyses is a premium service, requiring tuition to ensure sustainability and quality.

This program is not simply a collection of recorded lessons. They are the result of the coordinated effort of a team of over 100 professionals - tutors, teaching assistants, ambassadors across departments, coordinators and directors - who dedicate their expertise to ensure that all learners receive high-quality training once they enroll. The tuition fees secure the continuation of our program, support the many professionals working behind the scenes, and guarantee the high standards our participants expect.

The Application Process

Our programs are not designed for everyone. They require commitment, engagement, and alignment between the learner's goals and what we provide. For this reason, every prospective participant must go through an application and waiting list process:

01

Waiting list registration.

Interested applicants place their names on our official waiting list available online.

02

Direct Contact.

One of our commercial representatives reaches out to schedule a call when a spot becomes available.

03

Alignment call.

This conversation allows us to understand the applicant's ambitions, needs, and current reality, while also clarifying whether our program is the right fit.

This process ensures not only that the participant benefits from the training, but also that the Academy can provide what they need to succeed. The structure helps us maintain high-quality cohorts of learners who are committed to achieving meaningful outcomes.

THE VALUE OF THE INVESTMENT

Our premium program is designed to deliver profound professional transformation. Graduates gain practical skills, applied expertise, and a strong methodological foundation in meta-analysis - tools that often accelerate their careers and research impact. Thus, while our tuition represents an investment, it also reflects the exceptional value and long-term benefits that participants derive from the experience.
