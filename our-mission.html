Who we are

A global
research
community.

The Meta-Analysis Academy is a global training platform dedicated to advancing evidence synthesis and meta-analysis methodology across medical and surgical fields.

Our mission is to cultivate rigorous, ethical, and clinically relevant scholarly work by equipping healthcare students and professionals with the skills needed to design, conduct, and disseminate high-quality systematic reviews and meta-analyses. In under three years, our community has grown to learners across 150 countries.

Our Community

In under three years, our community has grown to learners across 150 countries spanning South America, North America, Europe, Asia, Africa, and Oceania. Our students, who consist mostly of medical students, interns, residents, and physicians, have authored or co-authored thousands of publications in peer-reviewed journals and presentations in international conferences throughout all medical specialties.

Our alumni have won research awards from the American Heart Association, the European Society of Medical Oncology, Endocrine Society, United European Gastroenterology Week, World Congress of Endoscopy, and countless other global societies.

In addition, they have published at the highest-ranking journals in nearly every specialty. These include JAMA Cardiology, Neurosurgery, Anaesthesia and Analgesia, Endoscopy, Ophthalmology, European Journal of Haematology, JAMA Pediatrics, American Journal of Obstetrics and Gynecology, Journal of the European Academy of Dermatology, European Radiology, Journal of Pediatric Surgery, Plastic and Reconstructive Surgery (PRS), and others.

We do NOT do publications, nor do we guarantee publications for students. We provide the training, support, and network for collaboration among students, but the intellectual work and responsibility for each publication are solely of study authors.

Our Ethical
Foundation.

We teach and uphold the highest standards in integrity, transparency, and accountability. While we provide structured education in methods, writing, journal selection, and submission strategies, all scholarly outputs remain the sole responsibility of the authors.

Integrity First

We reject all unethical practices in medical research — including the commercial sale of authorship, ghostwriting, and honorary authorship. Authorship must be earned through substantial, verifiable contributions.

International Standards

All practices are aligned with ICMJE standards. We encourage learners to exercise leadership by forming and managing their own research groups responsibly and independently.

Student Ownership

All scholarly outputs remain the sole responsibility of the authors. The Meta-Analysis Academy does not assume responsibility for unethical practices undertaken independently by individuals.
